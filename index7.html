How to cook an egg:

  1. Crack the egg.
  2. Pour its contents into the pan.
  3. Let it cook for a few minutes.
  4. Gently stir ("scramble") the egg.
  5. Cook the egg thoroughly.
  6. Remove from pan.
  7. Eat!
